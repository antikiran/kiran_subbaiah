Description:
CCTV Interception application. Records edits and plays back video footage instantly to create fictitious scenes from immediate occurances. The fiction fabricated by the interceptor is interwoven seemlessly with live footage captured by the camera.

Download:
MIRRORAGE.ZIP
Requirements: Windows OS, webcam.
How to: Unzip the package and open the "MIRRORAGE.exe" file.
Press "Z" after launch to activate interceptor. "H" for futher help.
