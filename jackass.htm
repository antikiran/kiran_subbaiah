Bell tinkle
Stay simple
Star twinkle
An inkling’s ample
To dimple down the pimple.


Hop off cradle
Sit at table
Sip from ladle
Wear a label
Saying ‘sound and able’.

Bloom in April
Be careful
Eve’s rosy apples
Tacked and stapled
Onto shaky steeples.

Scrape scribbles
Scrap the syllabus
Drop the dribble
Grow up dabble
In trippy babble.

Prop up paddles
Belt the saddles
Be baby Babel
Before jackass fable
Hooves down stable.
