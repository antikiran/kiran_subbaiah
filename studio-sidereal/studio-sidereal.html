Kiran Subbaiah / Net.art / STUDIO_SIDEREAL

Description: Studio Sidereal is a black-box recovered from the debris of a time-machine that crashed in the vicinity of the artist's space-time. It contains a vital SOS audio message from a distant future addressed specifically to the world of contemporary art.
