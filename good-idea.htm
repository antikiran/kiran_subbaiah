Kiran Subbaiah / Texts by KS / A Good Idea


I have an idea.

It is a good idea.

I tell it to you because it is a good idea.

It is good for you.

You are convinced that the idea is good.

I tell it to others.

They are all convinced that it is a good idea.

You and all the people I tell the idea to also tell it to others who tell it yet again to others….

Everybody is convinced that the idea is good.

The idea spreads to all corners of the world and is remembered forever.

But don’t forget –

Don’t ever forget to tell all the people you tell the idea to that it was I who told the idea to you.

It’s MY idea.
