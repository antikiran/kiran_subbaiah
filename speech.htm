All that I have to say...

Index:Texts by KS
 
 


 
 

All that I have to say is that

when there is the opportunity to express some thing...

on an occasion like this for example,

when a reader happens to be available....

I mean, when we look at it from the right point of view -

that is, the way we see it...

or the way it is actually...

or in fact, the way it appears...

I suppose you know what I mean -

any way, when we look at it from the right point of view,

I get this vague impression

that it can be explained with absolute certainty,

that the general idea of the whole point

lies in how it can be explained for sure

that the general idea of the whole...

I mean, since the whole point of what I'm trying to say

is to clarify the whole point of what is being said...

because I mean, for me, personally I think -

well, this is very personal -

but personally I think that what I think is Universal...

I mean the fact that it is absolutely important

that it doesn't really matter...

but since its already being taken into consideration,

I'm not sure as to exactly how it should be explained,

but I'm sure that you have understood.

Thank you.
