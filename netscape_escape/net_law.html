NETADJUSTICE
 

Netlink makes Mouse its food

Lion feeds on Mouse

as master should

yet neither authority nor submission

can be assumed

for he who's eaten

hasn't really been consumed

each survives

to keep the the other alive

 and if one were dead

the other isn't fed

each makes himself the other's food

only for his own good.

A long way Mouse has crept

the Promise promised shall be kept

to Lion - Mouse is dear

carry on - there's nothing to fear

with absolute trust

Mouse may access
