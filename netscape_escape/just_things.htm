ALL THINGS JUST

The number of Just things offered are infinite

but you'll find yours in a minute

rest assure, for it's here

that the greatest treasure lies secure.

The just request devoid of greed:

what's Wanted = what one Needs,

 and the just seeker learns as he seeks

that Everybody's quest is for the Unique.

 Rights of possession remain  unreserved

what you choose is what you deserve.
