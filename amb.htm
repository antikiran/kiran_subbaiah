Index: Kiran Subbaiah
 


 All My Belongings: Shown at the Rijksakademie van beeldende kunsten, Amsterdam from 27-11-2002 to 1-12-2002. A public display was made of everything that Kiran Subbaiah possessed at this time, except for works of art or things connected to the making of art. Each object bore a lable with the name of the artist and of the object.
